FAQ

  • What is your work experience and what are your achievements?

    I have over 8,000 hours of experience in web development, or in other words, over four years. During this time, I have successfully completed many projects: from creating simple websites to complex web applications. My work covers all aspects of web development, including frontend and backend, as well as integration with various systems and services. I am constantly improving my skills and strive to implement advanced technologies to create high-quality and reliable digital products.

  • What technologies do you know in the field of web development, and what tools do you use?

    My web development tech stack includes: HTML5, CSS3, SCSS, SASS, Gulp, BEM, JavaScript, jQuery, and the Vue.js framework using the Option API, Composition API, Vue Router, Vuex, and Pinia. I also have a working knowledge of Git and GitHub for version control of my code. On the backend, I use PHP and work with the CMS WordPress. My preferred editor is VS Code. For design and prototyping, I work with Figma, Photoshop, Adobe XD, Zeplin, and Sketch.

  • What projects are you capable of implementing and what level of complexity can you take on?

    I am able to implement projects of any complexity, starting from discussing all the details and creating a unique design to layout, integration with CMS and placing the finished project on hosting. My experience allows me to confidently work on a variety of tasks, whether it is creating simple landings, developing multi-page sites or complex web applications. I pay attention to every detail to ensure high quality and customer satisfaction at every stage of the project.

  • How quickly can you complete tasks and how do you manage deadlines?

    My goal is to always meet the shortest deadlines and not go over the deadline line. I strive to complete tasks as the customer needs, without compromising the quality of the work. My approach to time management includes clear planning, task prioritization and efficient use of resources. Thanks to this, I am always ready to accept the challenge and complete tasks promptly, without compromising the quality of the result.

  • Do you provide project consultations and support, and how do you organize communication with the customer?

    Yes, I am available almost 24/7 for consultations and project support. I provide continuous communication with the client and am always ready to answer questions or discuss project details. In addition, I regularly provide reports on the work done each day directly in messages. This helps the client to be aware of the current status of the project and ensures transparency in our interactions.
